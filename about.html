About Us!

Pleasure Island is located in New Hanover County, North Carolina.
The Islands Chamber Of Commerce was established in 1937 with it's first president being Robert Cronly Plummer.
Pleasure Island was named and built for people trying to get away and have a great time.
We have a contact page to answer any questions about visiting and any other concerns that you might have.
